Current Work

Parks&Parkas

We created this application to allow a user who is interested in the outdoors and the beauty of this country to look ahead and plan their adventures. Parks&Parkas allows a user to search for national parks based on state selected. They are then redirected to another page that color codes days that will have good weather. If they click on a day with good weather, they are recommended a campground and displays user important information such as camping sites available, ice, firewood, and if there is a camp store.
